# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone,
regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions
- Focusing feedback on the code, not the person
- Accepting that maintainers may close issues or decline PRs that are out of scope

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment of any kind
- Publishing private information without consent
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or persistent unwanted contact

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [arivera@riveraeng.com](mailto:arivera@riveraeng.com).
All reports will be reviewed and responded to. Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or
reject any contribution that violates this code, and to ban any contributor whose behavior is
harmful to the project.
